FALOODA

Ingredients

  • 500 ml fresh whole milk (chilled)
  • 2-3 Tbsp white sugar
  • 2 scoops vanilla or kulfi ice cream
  • Chopped nuts (pistachios, almonds) for garnish
  • Optional: Fruit jelly cubes, chopped fresh fruits, or tutti frutti

Instructions

  1. Add rose syrup
  2. Layer Ingredients
  3. Pour Milk
  4. Add Ice cream and garnish
  5. Mix gently and enjoy
